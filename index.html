Who We Are

Born to
Climb Over

GECO is a fully-remote streetwear and footwear brand based in Banská Bystrica, Slovakia. We design shoes and baggy streetwear for anyone who wants to stand out — from the streets to the summit.

Our white gecko logo represents adaptability, grip, and the ability to scale any surface. We partner with the biggest brands in the game to bring you exclusive drops you won't find anywhere else.
